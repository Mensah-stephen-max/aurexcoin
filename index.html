About Aurex

Aurex is a decentralized native coin combining the scarcity of Bitcoin, the utility of Ethereum, and the speed of Solana. Built with a hybrid consensus mechanism (PoW + PoS), Aurex ensures security, scalability, and energy efficiency. With up to 60,000 TPS and near-zero fees, it’s ready for mass adoption.
